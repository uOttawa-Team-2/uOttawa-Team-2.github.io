Style guide:

Header:
The header has a background color of #add8e6 (lightBlue). 
The text in the header will have a color of #00008b (darkblue).
The font-size of the text will be 1.2em.
The text will be aligned in the center with a word spacing of 3px.
The padding in the header will be of 14px and 16px.
The radius of the border will be of 10 px.

The navigation line will have an inline display with 0 padding and 0 margin 
It will also have a background color of lightBlue.
It's list-style-type is none.

When someone hovers over any of the 4 elements in the header, the fontbecomes bold.
When on a page, the corresponding element in the header will be underlined. 


Body:
The body text will have the color #f2f2f2 and be sans-serif font.
The paragraph text will have a size of 24px.
The major heading text (h1) will have a size of 40 px.
The minor heading text (h2) will have a size of 30 px.
The background color will be #021d40.
The image will have a width and height, based on the picture's original dimensions, which are 1600px by 1200px.


Footer:
The footer will have a background color of #deb887 (nurlywood) and the text will be in the color #f2f2f2
The top border will be of 1px and have the color #021d40
It's position will be fixed.
The width will be 100%

Buttons:
The buttons have a background color of #f2f2f2 and the text is #021d40.
